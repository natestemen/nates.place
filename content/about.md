# `whoami`

Hey, I'm nate (he/they).
I'm an open source developer, quantum computing practitioner, and mathematics enjoyer.

I like to work at the intersection of research and software, often using software to push research along, and turn research ideas into usable tools.
I also like to take ideas from advanced math/physics/computing and present them to general audiences to help spread the love of abstract thinking for the fun of it.

In my spare time I enjoy staying active (rock climbing, running, riding bikes (while trying not to get hit by cars)), fostering relationships with loved ones, and trying to [slow down](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slow_movement_(culture)).
